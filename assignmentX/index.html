Assignment X: Creating a Personality

Description:

This assignment will ask you to tell a story through the creation of a character rather than an entire storyline.
You will explore an environment through a character's eyes instead of telling a narrative, and having the player
learn about the story and the world through the lens of the character that you create. The narrative will be produced
and created by how the character interacts and reacts to the environment that they are in.

Assignment Purpose

The purpose of this assignment is to explore the world of subtle storytelling where players will not be given explicit
narrative about the game, but still be able to infer things about the world and story through interactions between
characters and the world.

Assignment Readings and References:

1: Creating a lively and realistic character in Pixar:

2: La Porte, Christopher, "The Temple of Storytelling: Character Creation" article
3: Game- Little Nightmares - Explore the world playing as a character and learn about the environment/world without dialogue
or explicit narrative to tell a compelling story
Link to Steam Store

Assignment Specification:

  1. Create a single main character that the player controls/plays through using an engine of your choice.
    Other characters may be created for the sake of interactions, but dialogue between characters should not
    be the narrative itself, but it should give subtle hints towards backstory or a narrative

  2. A narrative story or backstory of your choice that is to be discovered through interactions with the
    environment or other characters.

  3. General playtime of ~5 mins, with the lore/narrative being discovered and somewhat explained by the end of the game

  4. Text file to be turned in with the submission explaining the story that is implied in the environment and a list of the
    hints towards this story from the interactions in the game. Aim for around ~300 words.

Collaboration:

If you choose to collaborate on this assignment, create two different characters and have them interact in the same space to
discover a shared lore/story between the two. The general playtime should be ~7mins and the text file submission should cover more
interactions and specific events between the two characters.
